Who We Are

Pet Pavilion was born from the need to create a safe space that gathers all the main needs of the pets and the owners. We are a small animal clinic and wellness center that ensures your pet’s needs are catered for, all in one place.

We offer not only standard clinical veterinary services, but also, alternative treatments, physiotherapy, sportive medicine, regenerative medicine, and grooming. We’re also here to offer your dog with a fun and stimulating daycare experience with a spacious swimming pool. In addition, a retail shop and a pet taxi service complete our offer.
